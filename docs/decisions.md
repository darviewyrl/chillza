# Design Decisions

## 2026-06

### Hero Strategy

Decision:

Use approved artwork files instead of constructing hero sections from multiple floating PNG assets.

Reason:

The previous implementation felt AI-generated and lacked composition quality.

---

### Hero Title

Decision:

Use icon-hero.png as the visual campaign title.

Reason:

The title is part of the advertising artwork, not part of the navigation brand system.

---

### Brand Logo

Decision:

Do not use the original brand logo inside the hero.

Reason:

It duplicates the campaign title and weakens visual hierarchy.

---

### Social Icons

Decision:

Use official Facebook and Instagram icons.

Reason:

Professional appearance and stronger brand recognition.

---

### Team Section

Decision:

Present contributors as editorial profiles rather than dashboard cards.

Reason:

The website is a campaign microsite, not an admin panel.

---

### Typography

Decision:

Use strong display typography for headlines and cleaner typography for body content.

Reason:

Improve readability and reduce AI-generated appearance.

---

### Artwork Philosophy

Decision:

Use artwork as a campaign background and storytelling element.

Reason:

The approved artwork already contains the intended product composition.

The website should enhance the artwork, not compete with it.
